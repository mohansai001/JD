Sr ETL Developer – Datastage

Job Title: Sr ETL Developer – Datastage
Primary skills:   ETL, IBM DataStage, Data Warehouse.
Secondary skills: AWS S3, Unix Scripting, Oracle SQL.Mode of work: Work from Office
Experience: 4 to 6 Years.

About the job:
We are looking for a skilled ETL Datastage Developer to design, develop, and maintain scalable ETL processes. The role involves working closely with business teams to extract, transform, and load data from various sources into target databases. The developer will be responsible for ensuring the efficiency and scalability of ETL pipelines and adhering to business logic.

Responsibilities:
•	Design and develop DataStage jobs to extract, transform, and load data into data warehouses.
•	Collaborate with business teams and data modelers to understand ETL requirements.
•	Develop mapping documents and transformation rules based on source-to-target mapping.
•	Handle large data volumes efficiently using IBM Infosphere DataStage Designer, Director, and Administrator.
•	Read files from AWS S3 buckets and load them into target databases.
•	Perform unit testing and performance testing of DataStage jobs.
•	Create and maintain ETL testing documentation, including test cases, results, and issues.
•	Work closely with cross-functional teams to ensure seamless data integration.

Requirements:
Must Have:

•	4-6 years of experience in IBM DataStage Tool and ETL engineering.
•	Experience with SQL for Data Warehouse and Data Hub testing.
•	Familiarity with AWS S3, Unix Scripting, and Oracle SQL.
•	Experience with DataStage on cloud platforms like IBM Cloudpak for data.
•	Strong communication and problem-solving skills.
