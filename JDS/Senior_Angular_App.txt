Job Title: Senior Angular Developer
Role Overview:
As a Senior Angular Developer, you will be responsible for designing, developing, and optimizing scalable front-end applications using Angular. You will lead front-end development efforts, mentor junior developers, and collaborate with cross-functional teams to create high-performance and user-friendly applications.

Key Responsibilities:
Architect, develop, and maintain enterprise-level Angular applications using Angular (Angular 8+) and TypeScript.
Optimize UI/UX performance, responsiveness, and accessibility.
Design and implement reusable components, modules, and services following best practices.
Work closely with backend developers to integrate RESTful APIs and GraphQL services.
Implement state management solutions (RxJS, NgRx) for efficient data handling.
Conduct code reviews, mentor junior developers, and enforce coding standards.
Troubleshoot complex UI/functional issues and enhance application performance.
Stay updated with Angular best practices, new features, and front-end trends.
Collaborate with UI/UX designers to improve usability and user experience.
Work with DevOps teams to ensure smooth CI/CD deployment of front-end applications.
Key Skills:
Expertise in Angular (Angular 8+), TypeScript, JavaScript, HTML, and CSS.
Strong knowledge of RxJS, NgRx, and state management techniques.
Experience in RESTful API and GraphQL integration.
Proficiency in component-based architecture and Angular lifecycle hooks.
Familiarity with front-end performance optimization techniques.
Experience with unit testing frameworks (Jasmine, Karma).
Hands-on experience with Git, CI/CD pipelines, and Agile methodologies.
Strong problem-solving, debugging, and leadership skills.
Ability to collaborate with cross-functional teams and mentor junior developers.
