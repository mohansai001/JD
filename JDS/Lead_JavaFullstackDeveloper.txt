Lead Java Fullstack Developer
Role Overview: As a Lead Java Fullstack Developer, you will be responsible for the technical leadership of full-stack development initiatives, including architecture, design, and development of scalable, enterprise-level web applications. You will guide a team of developers and ensure the highest quality standards are met.

Key Responsibilities:

Lead the design and architecture of full-stack applications, including front-end and back-end components.
Provide technical direction, mentoring, and leadership to junior and senior developers.
Ensure the development of high-quality, performant, and scalable applications.
Collaborate with business stakeholders to define project requirements and technical solutions.
Establish and enforce coding best practices and code reviews.
Optimize the full application stack for performance and scalability.
Oversee the deployment of applications and manage CI/CD pipelines.
Key Skills:

Deep expertise in Java, Spring Boot, Hibernate, and modern JavaScript frameworks (Angular, React).
Strong experience in software architecture, design patterns, and microservices.
Familiarity with cloud platforms (AWS, Azure, GCP) and containerization (Docker, Kubernetes).
Experience with continuous integration, continuous deployment, and automated testing.
Excellent leadership, communication, and collaboration skills.
Strong understanding of security, scalability, and performance optimization.
