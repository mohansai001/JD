Job Title: Senior .NET Developer
Role Overview:
As a Senior .NET Developer, you will be responsible for designing, developing, and maintaining robust and scalable .NET applications. You will lead development efforts, mentor junior developers, and ensure best coding practices while collaborating with cross-functional teams to deliver high-quality solutions.

Key Responsibilities:
Architect, develop, and maintain enterprise-level .NET applications using C#, .NET Core, ASP.NET MVC, and Web API.
Lead development efforts, review code, and mentor junior developers.
Optimize application performance and ensure scalability and security.
Work with SQL Server, Entity Framework, and ORM tools for data management.
Collaborate with UI/UX teams to enhance user experience.
Integrate third-party APIs and cloud-based services.
Implement DevOps practices with CI/CD pipelines, automated testing, and deployment.
Troubleshoot complex technical issues and provide scalable solutions.
Stay updated with emerging .NET technologies and contribute to architectural decisions.
Key Skills:
Strong expertise in C#, .NET Core, ASP.NET MVC, Web API, and Entity Framework.
Advanced knowledge of SQL Server, database design, and optimization techniques.
Experience with front-end technologies like JavaScript, Angular, React, or Vue.js.
Proficiency in microservices architecture, cloud computing (Azure/AWS), and containerization (Docker, Kubernetes).
Strong understanding of design patterns, SOLID principles, and best coding practices.
Experience with CI/CD pipelines, Git, and automated testing frameworks.
Excellent problem-solving, debugging, and analytical skills.
Ability to lead projects and collaborate with cross-functional teams effectively.
