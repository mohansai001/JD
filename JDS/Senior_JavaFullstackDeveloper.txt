Senior Java Fullstack Developer
Role Overview: As a Senior Java Fullstack Developer, you will lead the development of both front-end and back-end components of web applications. You will be responsible for designing and implementing scalable, high-performance systems and mentoring junior developers.

Key Responsibilities:

Design and implement both back-end and front-end components using Java and modern front-end technologies.
Develop and maintain RESTful APIs and integrate with other services.
Optimize front-end performance and ensure responsive, mobile-friendly designs.
Lead the development of scalable, secure, and maintainable Java-based web applications.
Perform code reviews, mentor junior developers, and establish coding standards.
Collaborate with cross-functional teams to ensure requirements are met and applications are delivered on time.
Troubleshoot and resolve complex issues across the stack.
Key Skills:

Proficiency in Java, Spring Boot, Hibernate, and other Java-based technologies.
Expertise in front-end frameworks (Angular, React, or Vue.js).
Strong experience with RESTful APIs, microservices, and cloud technologies.
Familiarity with databases (SQL/NoSQL) and data modeling.
Experience in test-driven development (TDD) and automated testing frameworks.
Strong problem-solving and debugging skills.
Leadership skills, including mentoring junior developers.
