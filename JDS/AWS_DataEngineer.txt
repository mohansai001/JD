AWS Data Engineer
Job Title: AWS Data Engineer
Primary skills: AWS Stack – Glue, Lambda, Athena, S3; Python
Secondary skills: SQL, PySpark
Mode of work: Work from Office
Experience: 4-7 years of experience
About the job
We are seeking a skilled AWS Data Engineer with experience in developing modern data solutions on cloud-native platforms, especially using AWS. The role involves building scalable and reliable data pipelines, designing custom AWS solutions, and collaborating with stakeholders to ensure effective data governance. You will work closely with data scientists and analysts to ensure data availability for analytics and reporting while handling large volumes of data.

Responsibilities:
•	Collaborate with stakeholders to develop modern data solutions on AWS cloud platform.
•	Design and implement custom solutions to support data governance initiatives.
•	Create and enhance workflows, data dictionaries, and scripts to optimize governance processes.
•	Build and maintain scalable data pipelines using AWS services like S3, Glue, Lambda, and Athena.
•	Develop ETL processes for data ingestion, transformation, and loading into data lakes and warehouses.
•	Work closely with data scientists and analysts to ensure data availability for analytics.
•	Handle large volumes of data efficiently.
•	Understand and translate business requirements into technical solutions.
•	Develop mapping documents and transformation business rules.
Requirements - Must have:
•	Overall 4-7 years of experience with at least 3+ years as a data engineer with AWS as the cloud platform.
•	Experience with AWS services: S3, Athena, Glue, Lambda, and Python.
•	Proficiency in SQL, Python, and PySpark.
•	Strong knowledge of Data Warehousing concepts.
•	Familiarity with Agile/Scrum methodologies.
•	Excellent verbal and written communication skills.
