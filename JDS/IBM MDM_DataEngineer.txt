SSE - IBM MDM Developer

The role involves working closely with technical teams to analyze requirements, validate data, and develop solutions to meet business needs.

Responsibilities:
•	Understand product vision and technical solution architecture.
•	Analyze requirements and provide technical approaches for tickets, reviewing with Tech Lead.
•	Provide Low Level Design (LLD) as required and perform unit testing.
•	Create and maintain development artifacts, and perform peer code reviews.
•	Fix issues and validate data in databases as part of release activities.
•	Support release activities and ensure quality deliverables.
Requirements - Must Have:
•	3-5 years of total IT experience, with 2+ years as an IBM MDM Advanced Edition developer (Preferably 11.x).
•	Strong understanding of master data management concepts and IBM MDM data models.
•	Proficiency in Oracle SQL and database query handling.
•	Strong debugging and problem-solving skills.
•	Strong written and verbal communication skills.
