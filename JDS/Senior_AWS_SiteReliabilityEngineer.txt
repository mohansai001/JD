Senior AWS Site Reliability Engineer
Role Overview:
As a Senior AWS Site Reliability Engineer, you’ll be responsible for optimizing the reliability and performance of AWS systems. You’ll focus on automation, monitoring, and proactive incident response to maintain high availability.

Key Responsibilities:

Design and implement monitoring, alerting, and logging solutions with AWS CloudWatch, ELK Stack, and Prometheus.
Automate routine tasks, deployment, and incident response using Infrastructure as Code (IaC) with Terraform or CloudFormation.
Perform capacity planning, performance tuning, and infrastructure optimization.
Lead incident management and post-mortem reviews to prevent recurring issues.
Collaborate with DevOps and development teams to enforce reliability best practices.
Key Skills:

Strong knowledge of AWS services and architecture (EC2, RDS, Lambda, S3, CloudWatch).
Proficiency in Infrastructure as Code (Terraform, CloudFormation) and CI/CD automation.
Advanced scripting skills (Python, Bash) for operational automation.
Experience with monitoring and alerting tools like CloudWatch, ELK, Grafana.
Knowledge of incident management, root-cause analysis, and proactive reliability measures.
