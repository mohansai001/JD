Job Title: Senior Mendix Developer
Role Overview:
As a Senior Mendix Developer, you will be responsible for designing, developing, and optimizing enterprise-level low-code applications using the Mendix platform. You will lead development efforts, mentor junior developers, and collaborate with business teams to deliver scalable and efficient applications.

Key Responsibilities:
Architect, develop, and maintain scalable Mendix applications for business solutions.
Design and implement data models, microflows, and page logic in Mendix.
Integrate Mendix applications with REST/SOAP APIs, databases, and third-party services.
Optimize performance, security, and usability of Mendix applications.
Lead code reviews, enforce best practices, and mentor junior developers.
Work with business analysts to translate requirements into technical solutions.
Troubleshoot and resolve complex application issues.
Stay updated with Mendix platform updates and low-code best practices.
Collaborate with cross-functional teams to ensure seamless project delivery.
Key Skills:
Expertise in Mendix platform and low-code development methodologies.
Strong knowledge of Mendix domain models, microflows, and security settings.
Experience in RESTful API and database integration.
Understanding of CI/CD pipelines and cloud deployment for Mendix applications.
Familiarity with Java, JavaScript, or SQL for advanced customizations.
Experience with unit testing, debugging, and performance tuning in Mendix.
Strong problem-solving, debugging, and leadership skills.
Ability to collaborate with stakeholders, business teams, and developers.
