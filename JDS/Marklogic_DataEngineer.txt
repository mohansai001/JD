Job Title: Sr. Developer – MarkLogic
Primary skills:   NoSQL: MarkLogic, XQuery, MongoDB, Cassandra

About the job
We are looking for a Sr. Developer with 4-7 years of experience in NoSQL databases, focusing on MarkLogic. The candidate should have strong technical expertise in XML, XPath, XQuery, and XSLT, and a deep understanding of unit testing, code versioning, and best practices. The role involves working closely with teams to design and implement solutions using MarkLogic Data Hub Framework, MLCP, and REST APIs.

Responsibilities:
•	Understand functional/non-functional requirements and participate in client calls to clarify requirements.
•	Analyze requirements and create impact analysis documents for understanding existing functionality changes.
•	Utilize NoSQL databases with a focus on MarkLogic, including data ingestion and harmonization.
•	Develop solutions using XML, XPath, XQuery, and XSLT for content management and bulk transformations.
•	Collaborate with Agile teams to deliver high-quality solutions, focusing on unit testing and best practices.

Requirements:
Must Have:

•	4-7 years of experience in NoSQL databases, with strong knowledge in MarkLogic and XML-based languages (XPath, XQuery, XSLT).

•	Experience working in an Agile environment with excellent verbal and written communication skills.
