Responsibilities:
•	Proficient in development of various dashboards, reports utilizing Tableau Visualizations like bar graphs, scatter plots, pie - charts, creating calculated fields/columns, geographic visualization and other making use of actions, other local and global filters according to the end user requirement.
•	Collect, import, analyze, and transform data from various sources for business intelligence purposes.
•	Interpret and translate complex business requirements into technical specifications and reports.
•	Managed, updated and manipulated report orientation and structures with the use of advanced Excel functions Pivot Tables and V-Lookups
•	Posses’ strong knowledge of database management in writing complex SQL queries, Stored procedures, database tuning, query optimization and resolving key performance issues
