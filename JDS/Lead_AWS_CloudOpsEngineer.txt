Job Description: Lead CloudOps Engineer

We are seeking a highly skilled Lead CloudOps Engineer to manage and optimize cloud infrastructure, ensuring high availability, scalability, and security. In this role, you will lead a team of engineers, automate deployment pipelines, monitor system performance, and resolve cloud infrastructure issues.

Key Responsibilities:

Design and implement robust cloud architecture.
Manage and optimize CI/CD pipelines for seamless deployments.
Ensure system reliability, scalability, and security compliance.
Automate infrastructure tasks using tools like Terraform or Ansible.
Lead incident management, root cause analysis, and performance tuning.
Mentor and guide a team of CloudOps engineers.
Qualifications:

Proven experience in cloud platforms (AWS, Azure, GCP).
Expertise in IaC tools (Terraform, CloudFormation).
Strong knowledge of DevOps practices and containerization (Docker, Kubernetes).
Excellent problem-solving and leadership skills.
